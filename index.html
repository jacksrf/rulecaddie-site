Fair questions.

Are the answers official rulings?

No — they're AI-generated with citations to the official Rules of Golf, and they're built to be right. For competition, confirm with a rules official. RuleCaddie is not affiliated with the USGA or The R&A.

Does it work without cell service?

The complete rule book summary works fully offline. On newer iPhones with Apple Intelligence, basic questions can even be answered by on-device AI with zero signal.

Is there an Apple Watch app?

Yes — ask from your wrist and the answer comes back through your phone. Your round also lives on the lock screen and in the Dynamic Island.

How does the handicap stuff work?

Enter your handicap index once. Photograph any course's scorecard and RuleCaddie reads the slope and rating, computes your course handicap with the official formula, and marks your stroke holes.

How do I cancel?

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Cancel anytime; Pro stays active until the period ends.
