Common questions

Why does the app say "iPhone not reachable" on my watch?
The Apple Watch app relays questions through your iPhone. Keep your phone nearby with RuleCaddie installed.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → RuleCaddie Pro → Cancel. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the paid period.

How do I delete my account?
Email us from the address you signed up with and we'll delete your account and all data within 30 days.

Are the answers official rulings?
No. Answers are AI-generated with citations to the official Rules of Golf. For competition, confirm with a rules official. RuleCaddie is not affiliated with the USGA or The R&A.
