Privacy Policy

RuleCaddie · Effective July 11, 2026

RuleCaddie ("the app") helps golfers get answers about the Rules of Golf. This policy explains what data the app handles and why. The short version: we collect the minimum needed to answer your questions and sync your account, we don't sell data, and we don't use it for advertising.

What we collect

  • Account information. Your email address (or Apple-provided relay address if you use Sign in with Apple), used to create and secure your account.
  • Questions and conversations. The rules questions you ask and the answers you receive are stored in your account so your history syncs across devices.
  • Photos you choose to share. If you photograph a local rules sheet, scorecard, or your ball's position, the image is processed to answer your question or extract course information. Extracted text (not the photo itself) may be saved to your course profiles.
  • Round data. Courses, holes, penalties, and handicap you log in Round Mode.
  • Approximate location (optional). Only if you grant permission, used on-device to suggest nearby golf courses. Your location is not stored on our servers.

How AI answers work

When you ask a question, its text (and any photo you attach) is sent to our server and forwarded to Anthropic, the AI provider that generates the answer. Anthropic processes this data to provide the response and does not use API data to train its models. Voice input is transcribed on your device by Apple's speech recognition; audio is not sent to our servers.

Subscriptions and payments

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. We receive only a transaction identifier used to verify your subscription status.

Where data lives

Account data is stored with Supabase (our database provider) with row-level security, meaning your data is accessible only to your authenticated account.

What we don't do

  • No selling or sharing of personal data with data brokers
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

Data deletion

You can delete individual conversations in the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, email us at the address below and we'll complete the deletion within 30 days.

Children

RuleCaddie is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update this page and note the new effective date above.
